Cellesto's Features

All-inclusive yet Uncomplicated.

School Admin

Transform your school/institution into an well-oiled all-integrated, scalable, flexible Academic powerhouse, by managing all facets with unforeseen ease.

Academics

  • Add Academics Session, Curriculum Type, Class, sections & Subjects for School/Institute with ease.
  • Create ‘Class combination’ i.e. Combination of curriculum + class + section.

Customized Marksheet Template

  • Can create/manage Marksheet format/template as per prevalent/needed by School.
  • Publish Marksheet for Students online on Cellesto and can get the Printouts.

Leave Management

  • Add/Manage ‘Leave Type’ as per Leave policy, with number of leaves for that category.
  • Check leaves applied by Students and Teachers.
  • Approve or reject Teachers Leaves.
  • Students leaves can be approved by class teacher as well as school admin.

Manage Students

  • Add/Manage Students, with Personal | Parent | Class | Transport | Qualification | Contact details, etc.
  • Automatic login credential for student & parent, once a student profile is created.
  • Upload student data in bulk, with pre-defined as well as School’s own excel format.

Fee Management with Online Fee Submission

  • Defining of ‘Fee Heads’ for whole School/Institute.
  • Allocate Fee Heads for curriculum and class with fee structure including Penalties, Discount/Scholarship.
  • Parents can pay Fees via Cash/DD/Cheque/’Bank Challan’ at School or Bank Counter, with auto-calculated Due Fee details, and then generate Fee Invoice.

Exams

  • Defining ‘Exam Head’ i.e. Class Test, Monthly Exam, Quarterly Exam, etc.
  • Define class-wise schedule with selection of subject, date, starting time, ending time and required Min. passing marks.
  • Defining syllabus of exam available to parent/teacher/student to prepare.
  • School admin can Give Marks or Check Marks given by teacher with system calculated Grade as per obtained marks for exams completed by student.
